Long-Term Investment Philosophy & Strategy

At Sustainable Growth Income, we believe in the power of long-term investing, focusing on businesses with solid foundations and sustainable growth potential. Our philosophy is based on:

  • In-depth fundamental analysis: Thoroughly researching financial conditions, competitive advantages, leadership teams, and industry outlooks.
  • Patience and strategic vision: Investing is not a short-term game. We encourage persistence in investments with real intrinsic value.
  • Tight risk management: Constructing diversified portfolios tailored to each client's risk appetite and financial goals.
  • Smart diversification: Allocating assets across various types such as stocks, bonds, funds, and real estate to optimize returns and mitigate risks.
  • Growth-oriented partnership: We not only provide recommendations but also closely monitor and adjust portfolios as needed to stay aligned with market movements and client objectives.

We prioritize industries with long-term growth potential such as technology, healthcare, essential consumer goods, and renewable energy. Our stock selection criteria include competent and transparent leadership, sustainable competitive advantages, and attractive valuations.

Vnstock

A powerful Python library that provides access to historical and real-time data of the Vietnamese stock market. It supports technical analysis, statistics, and quantitative modeling. Especially useful for individual and institutional investors seeking to automate analysis workflows.

FiinPro

A comprehensive and in-depth financial data platform for the Vietnamese market. FiinPro offers detailed information on companies, sectors, and macroeconomics, along with powerful stock screening and analysis tools. Ideal for analysts and investment funds.

Python & Jupyter Notebook

A powerful duo for data analysis and quantitative finance. Python, along with libraries such as Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, and Scikit-learn, enables advanced data processing, visualization, and modeling. Jupyter Notebook provides an interactive environment for writing code, displaying results, and documenting analysis.
